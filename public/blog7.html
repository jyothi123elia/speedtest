🧘 100 Best Buddha Quotes That Will Change Your Life

Buddha's wisdom has guided millions of people for over 2500 years. These powerful quotes carry timeless truth that is just as relevant in our modern world today as it was centuries ago.

Quotes on Peace

"Peace comes from within. Do not seek it without."

— Buddha

"If you are quiet enough, you will hear the flow of the universe. You will feel its rhythm. Go with this flow. Happiness lies ahead."

— Buddha

"Do not dwell in the past, do not dream of the future, concentrate the mind on the present moment."

— Buddha

"The mind is everything. What you think you become."

— Buddha

Quotes on Happiness

"Happiness never decreases by being shared."

— Buddha

"There is no path to happiness. Happiness is the path."

— Buddha

"Thousands of candles can be lit from a single candle and the life of the candle will not be shortened. Happiness never decreases by being shared."

— Buddha

"If you truly loved yourself, you could never hurt another."

— Buddha

Quotes on Patience

"Be patient. Everything comes to you at the right time."

— Buddha

"It does not matter how slowly you go as long as you do not stop."

— Buddha

"A jug fills drop by drop."

— Buddha

"You will not be punished for your anger. You will be punished by your anger."

— Buddha

Quotes on Success

"Work out your own salvation. Do not depend on others."

— Buddha

"The secret of health for both mind and body is not to mourn for the past, worry about the future, or anticipate troubles, but to live in the present moment wisely and earnestly."

— Buddha

"However many holy words you read, however many you speak, what good will they do you if you do not act on them?"

— Buddha

"Three things cannot be long hidden: the sun, the moon, and the truth."

— Buddha

Quotes on Love and Kindness

"You yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection."

— Buddha

"Radiate boundless love towards the entire world — above, below, and across — unhindered, without ill will, without enmity."

— Buddha

"In the end, only three things matter: how much you loved, how gently you lived, and how gracefully you let go of things not meant for you."

— Buddha

"If you light a lamp for somebody, it will also brighten your path."

— Buddha

Quotes on Change

"Nothing is forever except change."

— Buddha

"If you do not change direction, you may end up where you are heading."

— Buddha

"Every morning we are born again. What we do today is what matters most."

— Buddha

"The root of suffering is attachment."

— Buddha
