What is Download Speed?

Download speed is how fast data comes from the internet to your device. This affects how quickly you can load websites, stream videos, and download files. Most online activities depend on download speed.

What is Upload Speed?

Upload speed is how fast data goes from your device to the internet. This matters for video calls, uploading files to Google Drive, live streaming, and sending emails with large attachments.

What is Ping?

Ping measures the delay between your device and the server in milliseconds. Lower ping is better especially for online gaming and video calls.

Average Internet Speed in India 2025

The average fixed broadband speed in India is around 80-100 Mbps in 2025. Mobile internet averages around 30-50 Mbps on 4G and 100-200 Mbps on 5G networks.
