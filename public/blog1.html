Why is My Internet Slow? Top 10 Reasons

Is your internet running slow? Here are the top reasons and how to fix them.

1. Too Many Devices Connected

When many devices share one connection, speed drops for everyone. Disconnect unused devices.

2. Router Placed Badly

Place your router in the center of your home for best signal coverage.

3. Old Router

Routers older than 3 years may not support fast speeds. Consider upgrading.

4. Peak Hours

Internet is slower during 8PM-11PM when everyone is online. This is normal.

5. Background Downloads

Apps updating in background consume your bandwidth. Check and pause them.
